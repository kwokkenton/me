Heart Failure Predictions | August 2020

Predicting the likelihood of a person having heart failure would be extremely valuable to the medical field as early diagnosis would enhance the possibility of sound recovery. An XGBoost model was used to model whether a person would suffer from fatal heart failure and an F1-score of ~73% was consistently achieved.

Technologies and relevant packages: Python, Numpy, Pandas, Scikit-learn
Skills: Data analysis, Machine Learning, Model selection, Hyperparameter optimisation

Interferometry/ Spectroscopy Correction Algorithm | 6 Nov 2020

This was a Year 2 Physics Laboratory extension where we experimented with an inteferometer, which is an instrument capable of using light to measure lengths on the order of its wavelength. I developed an algorithm to correct for the jitter caused by mechanical movement of a mirror. This was compared against the known emission spectrum of Mercury.

Technologies and relevant packages: Python, Numpy, Seaborn
Skills: Experimentation, Data Analysis, Algorithm Development
